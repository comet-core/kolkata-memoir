After two days in Narendrapur, it was time for my mom and me to pack up and head back to Sodepur. The time with my aunt had been exactly what we needed, but we were ready to head back.

Before making the full trip, we took a detour to South City Mall for a quick lunch. It was nice to just step into a massive, air-conditioned bubble of normal city life for a bit. Seeing the towering high-rises and the bustling food court felt like a total reset.

We finally got back to Sodepur around 4:30 or 5:00 in the afternoon. I expected to walk back into a packed, chaotic house, but it was actually pretty quiet and relaxed. The intense weight of the first few days had definitely started to lift, and the house was settling into a much lighter, more comfortable rhythm. 

By 10:00 PM, a few of us were just chilling in the AC room, winding down for the night. The mood had shifted enough that the fun was starting to creep back in.
